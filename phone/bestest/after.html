You come back after school to take care of your sick friend. You barge in, not even bothering to ring the doorbell. Zim gets up from the couch. "I've been expecting you." And walks towards you. "There's something important I have to tell you, so listen closely." He puts his mouth next to your ear. "I DON'T WANT TO BE YOUR FRIEND ANYMORE!" Your ears are ringing so loud, you can barely hear your heart breaking! You run home, crushed.

The End
