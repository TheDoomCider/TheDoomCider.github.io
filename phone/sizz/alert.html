With the Great Foodening looming, you are unable to pursue Zim. Instead you contact the Tallest. They are busy preparing for the great assigning, so one of their servants answers your call. You inform him that Zim escaped and is headed straight for Conventia. Startled by the news, he quickly alerts the guards. When Zim reaches Conventia he is apprehended and returned to you on Foodcourtia. Angered by his escape, you forbid him from taking breaks and never let him out of your sight.
